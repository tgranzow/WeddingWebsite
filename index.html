Our Love Story

Where it all began

In February of 2015, we both decided to try online dating. Neither of us believed there was any chance it would work, and we both tried it with low expectations. And then through the magic of OKCupid, I received a message from Jason. We had an instant connection. We made plans for a few weeks out, but less than a week later, we just had to see each other.

On February 27, we met at the Marcus Majestic Movie Theater for our first date. We saw the movie Kingsman, a horrible movie, but the company was amazing. A week later, we had our second date, and from there, it was impossible to stop how quickly we fell for each other. By September of 2015, our little family found and moved into our cozy home.
